## Overview
Presentations are all about story-telling. Dictate what you reveal and when.
Use the reveal component to dictate what to reveal and when.

## Installation
<InstallCommand packageName="slide-cn@reveal" />

## Usage
```tsx
<Reveal>
	This content will be hidden until clicked
</Reveal>
```
