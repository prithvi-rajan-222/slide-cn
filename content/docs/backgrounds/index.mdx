---
title: Backgrounds
description: Ready-made background components for your slides
---

Backgrounds are components you pass to the `background` prop on `<Slide>`. They render behind your slide content and adapt to your active theme.

- [Gradient Backgrounds](./backgrounds/gradients) — `LinearGradient` and `RadialGradient`
- [Mesh Gradient](./backgrounds/mesh) — `MeshGradient`
- [Conic Gradient](./backgrounds/conic) — `ConicGradient`
- [Spotlight Gradient](./backgrounds/spotlight) — `SpotlightGradient`
- [Striped Background](./backgrounds/stripes) — `StripedGradient`
- [Noise Gradient](./backgrounds/noise) — `NoiseGradient`
